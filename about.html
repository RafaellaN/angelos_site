About Us

Stavroucpa is an established audit firm, comprising by a group of talented accountants having the higher academic degrees, professional qualifications and working experience. We are specialized in the field of Audit, Assurance, Business Advisory services, VAT compliance and Corporate and Personal Tax matters. We are a fully licensed audit and accounting Service Provider firm regulated by the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Cyprus (ICPAC).

Our Vision

Stavroucpa vision is to become one of the best audit and tax service provider firm of the island and even beyond. Managements vision is for the firm to be well established in the Cyprus market and be one of the most preferred service providers.

Meet the Team

ANGELOS STAVROU (FCCA)

MANAGING DIRECTOR

Angelos graduated from Oxford Brookes University in 2007 with a Bachelor Degree in Applied Accounting. Became member of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) in 2005 and a member of the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Cyprus (ICPAC) in 2006. Within the years 2005 to 2009 he worked with two of the Big4 audit firms, specialized in the field of Audit and Assuance services. In the year 2015, Angelos received his MBA from Cyprus Institute of Management, receiving the YPH award for achieving outstanding academic performance. Held various positions in ICPAC committees along the years, served as a CFO of a conglomerate group of Companies for more than 12 years.

Angelos is specialised in the field of Audit and Assurance and strategic Tax planning.
